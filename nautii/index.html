About nautii

Fusing the relentless energy of house and dance with the raw storytelling of hip-hop, nautii is the new sound of Miami's vibrant nightlife. Her music is a playful, sensual invitation to the dance floor.

From late-night studio sessions to commanding the stage at the city's hottest clubs, nautii's journey is one of pure passion and dedication to the beat.
