About nautii

Born under the neon glow of Miami, I live and breathe the sultry pulse of the 305. My world is one of sticky summer nights, bass you can feel in your bones, and the intoxicating freedom of being completely, unapologetically yourself.

I make anthems for the after-hours, for the moments when you feel your most alive and your most desired. This isn't just music you listen to; it's a mood you slip into when the sun goes down and your inhibitions follow.

Feel the Pulse

Think of my sound as the soundtrack for your most unforgettable nights. Each track is built on slinky basslines, shimmering synths, and vocals that feel like a secret being whispered in your ear on a crowded dance floor. It's music designed to make you move, to make you feel sexy, and to celebrate the power in your own sensuality. So press play, turn it up, and let go; this is your invitation to the party.
